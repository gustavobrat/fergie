THE DOUBLE DUTCHESS PROJECT

Released in 2017, Double Dutchess is the second studio album by American singer Fergie. It marks her first independent release under her own label, Dutchess Music. The project was conceived as a "Visual Experience," where each track is accompanied by a high-fashion music video directed by Bruno Ilogti and creative directed by Giovanni Bianco.

The aesthetic merges black-and-white noir photography with digital "glitch" textures, exploring themes of fame, motherhood, and resilience.
